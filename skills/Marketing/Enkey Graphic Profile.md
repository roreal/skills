## Logo Variants

The logo is available in three color variants (black, dark tone, light tone) and three formats (square/icon, landscape, horizontal), as well as a white variant on a black background.

---

### Square / Icon (319×319 px)

Used when the logo is displayed in a small size or in a square format, e.g. profile pictures and favicons.

| Variant | Color | Image |
|---------|------|
| Black | `#000000` | – |
| Dark tone | `#5C1F99` | ![Enkey logo square dark tone](images/enkey-grafisk-profil-logotyp-mork-kvadrat.png) |
| Light tone | `#8A1F99` | ![Enkey logo square light tone](images/enkey-grafisk-profil-logotyp-ljus-kvadrat.png) |

---

## Image files

All logo files are located in the `images/` folder and are named according to the convention:

```
enkey-grafisk-profil-logotyp-{färg}-{format}.png
```
